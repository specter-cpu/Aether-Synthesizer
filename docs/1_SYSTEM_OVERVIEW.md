## 1. Executive System Overview
The **Aether-Synthesizer** is a high-performance quantitative trading backtest, simulation, and execution engine designed for algorithmic style verification. It acts as an advanced mathematical playground that processes institutional strategy setups—such as the *ICT Silver Bullet Model* or *SMC Liquidity Grab*—against real-world historical market feeds across key asset domains (Foreign Exchange, Commodities, Digital Assets, and Equity Indices).

By translating market mechanics and strategic parameters into a pure deterministic framework, the system bridges the gap between theoretical risk-to-reward metrics and practical performance curves. The primary objective of Aether-Synthesizer is to provide traders and competition committees with an objective, high-fidelity projection of strategy viability, eliminating psychological distortion and evaluating equity trends under strict friction profiles.
